# Privacy Policy for ScrollSync

**Effective Date:** March 3, 2026

ScrollSync ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the Extension handles information when you use it.

## 1. Data Collection and Usage
ScrollSync is a client-side tool designed for automated page navigation and accessibility. 
- **No Personal Data Collection:** The Extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, browsing history, or sensitive user data to any external servers.
- **Local Storage:** Any user-defined settings (such as scroll speed or gamepad configurations) are stored locally within your browser using the standard `chrome.storage` API. This data never leaves your device.
- **Permissions:** The Extension utilizes the `activeTab`, `scripting`, and `storage` permissions solely to perform scrolling, clicking, and navigation actions on the web pages you explicitly interact with.

## 2. Third-Party Sharing
We do not share, sell, or trade any user information with third parties. There are no analytics or tracking scripts embedded in the Extension.

## 3. Data Security
Because all data remains local to your browser, the security of your information is protected by your browser's built-in security features.

## 4. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this page.

## 5. Contact
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact the developer through the official repository or store support page.
